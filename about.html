About the Project

Healthcare Redesign is an independent policy initiative driven by the urgent need for structural reform in the United States healthcare system.

This project stems from the recognition that current legislative proposals address only the symptoms of cost inflation—pricing secrecy, billing disparities, and administrative waste—without curing the underlying disease: the separation of the consumer from the financial transaction.

This framework is submitted not as an academic exercise, but as a practical blueprint for restoring market discipline to a sector that has lost it.
